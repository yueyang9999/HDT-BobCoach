# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Project discussions must be respectful, specific, and focused on improving the software. Reasoned disagreement, technical criticism, and requests for evidence are welcome. Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized content, personal attacks, doxxing, and deliberate disclosure of private data are not.

## Scope

This standard applies to Issues, pull requests, code review, discussions, and other spaces where a person represents the project.

## Reporting

Do not disclose sensitive conduct reports in a public Issue. Until a private project contact is published, use GitHub's built-in abuse-reporting tools for urgent platform violations. Maintainers may remove content, lock threads, reject contributions, or restrict participation when necessary to protect contributors and users.

Enforcement decisions should identify the behavior at issue, preserve relevant evidence, and avoid publishing unnecessary personal information.
